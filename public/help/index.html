Help: How to Use MTA Bus Time

Click It

Find out where buses are using a map on any computer or tablet by visiting mta.info/bustime in a web browser.

Scan It

Scan the QR code at your bus stop and find out how many stops away the next bus is. Or visit mta.info/bustime using a mobile web browser on your Android, iPhone, Blackberry or other smartphone.
