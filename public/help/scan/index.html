Scan It

Each bus stop has its own unique QR (Quick Response) code. The QR code is located on the bottom of the new bus schedules with the MTA Bus Time logo at top. Just scan it using a smartphone equipped with a QR reader app and get information on how many stops away the next bus is.

Or just visit mta.info/bustime using a mobile web browser on your Android, iPhone, Blackberry or other smartphone. Search for stops and routes nearby using your phone's GPS, or enter a bus route or nearby intersection in the search box. For more detailed instructions on using MTA Bus Time on your smartphone, click here.
