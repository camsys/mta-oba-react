The MTA Bus Time map shows you where buses are along their routes.

Using the Bus Time search box, type in an intersection, bus route or bus stop code, or zoom in on the map. See where buses are in real-time and select the bus nearest your stop.

Want to know your bus stop code? Search for the nearest intersection, or search for your route and scan one of its directions for your stop. Or zoom in and click the stop on the map.

Click on the arrow in the lower left corner of the MTA Bus Time home page for a tutorial on how to use the website.
