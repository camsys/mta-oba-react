About the New Bus Time Beta

Try the new MTA Bus Time (beta)

The redesigned MTA Bus Time makes it faster and easier to check bus arrivals and plan your trips. With a unified, responsive layout, you’ll get the same smooth experience whether you’re on your phone, tablet, or computer, as well as features that were previously only available on desktop.

For the first time, an interactive map is now available on mobile. Press and hold on the map to quickly find nearby stops and routes; on desktop, right-click anywhere on the map to do the same. Wherever you are, it’s easier than ever to find your bus.

We’ve also added new ways to help you plan smarter and travel more comfortably. Save your favorite stops and routes for quick access, see bus crowding information before you head out, and move seamlessly between views with improved navigation.

Performance and reliability have been upgraded behind the scenes, so the information you need is there when you need it.

Try it now and share your feedback—we’re excited to hear what you think!
